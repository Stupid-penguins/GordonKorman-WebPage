< G O R D O N K O R M A N >

Gordon Korman is a Canadian American author.
Korman has written 100 children's and young adult
fiction books. Korman's books have sold more than
30 million copies worldwide over a career spanning
four decades and have appeared at number one on
The New York Times Best Seller list

When a fall from a roof wipes out Chase Ambrose's memory, he has to restart his life.
Upon returning to school, Chase is puzzled by the differences in the way that others treat him.
Some are happy to see him while others steer clear of him.Can Chase figure out who he was before?
